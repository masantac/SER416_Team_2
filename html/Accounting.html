Accounting

Do you enjoy technology, math, and working with people? Then our accounting position may be a perfect fit for you.

As an Accountant, you are the most important element of our financial backend. This is a fantastic opportunity for a self-starter with strong management skills. Ready to grow your Accounting & Finance career?

 

In this position, you will:

  • Perform, or oversee, Quickbooks reconciliations for multiple clients
  • Design and oversee the accounts payable process for clients
  • Generate reports, review, create observations, and present to Account Managers
  • Understand general financial performance for all clients
  • Interface with CPA's to provide information to prepare taxes

Role requirements:

  • Bachelor's Degree Required or three years of relevant work experience.
  • Strong knowledge of Excel
  • Confidence in Quickbooks (have support of TTG Controller and external CPA)
  • Excellent communications and customer service skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills combined with determination, resiliency and a positive attitude

We offer:

  • A competitive pay – base plus weekly performance bonus
  • Full benefits – health, dental, vision, 401K
  • 25 days of paid time off
  • Travel opportunities to our many offices throughout the US
  • Casual and fun work environment

If you have excellent communication and people skills, strong leadership capabilities, great work ethic, willingness to succeed, and a passion for helping charities succeed…. then WE WANT TO HEAR FROM YOU!
