Welcome to the Awesome Community Site

This site provides you with all the information and services that you need. The services that the community center offers are found below which also have a description of what those services are.

Create An Event

If you are looking to create an event for your friends, family or the community then this service is for you. It will allow you to create an event at the community center. You will need to fill out the appropriate information to ensure that you have a space reserved for your event.

Create an Event »

Volunteer For An Event

If you are interested in becoming a volunteer for an event or to volunteer at the community center then this is for you. You will be allowed to choose an event or day to volunteer for while providing the necessary information to make sure you have your spot reserved.

Volunteer »

Equipment Rental

If you are looking into renting equipment, such as lighting, tables, chairs, hardware and much more then this is for you. With this service you will be allowed to rent and reserve equipment for you to use. You will be asked to fill out an appropriate form to ensure availability.

Rent Equipment »

Calendar Of Events

In this page, you will find a calendar that allows you to view all of the events that are taking place at the community center. These events are, but not limited to, events, classes, fund raisers, drives. You will also be able to view the details including the time and date.

Calendar »

Make A Donation

If you want to make a difference in the community and contribute to the growth then a donation is something you would want to make. You will be given a form to fill out which also gives you options of how much you would like to donate.

Donate »

Customize Your Profile

In this section, you will be able to modify your profile to be up to date so you wont miss any important newsletters or notification from the community center.

Manage Profile »
